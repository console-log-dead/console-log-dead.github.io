Who am I?

I'm a year 1 student in Singapore Polytechnic currently studying in School of Computing's Diploma in Applied AI & Analytics.

Since young, I have had an interest in buses and photography. As a bus enthusiast, I chase after unique bus models, advertisements and/or deployments and photograph them. Even when I'm overseas, I do try and make some time to photograph whatever's interesting in the country.

I also do cosplay photography for my friends, which involves me planning storyboards and references, going to different places for photoshoots and going to many events with them, such as Anime Festival Asia & Cosfest.

I mainly use my Sony RX-10 iv as my main camera and a Fujifilm X-E1 with a Fuji XF50mm f/2 R WR as my side camera.
